It is March 30th 2014 when the Ukrainian store owner Nikolaj 'Kolja' Nikolajevitsj Temnikov gets into his car to take a short trip into town. All of a sudden two men also get in the car. His niece Yulia witnesses the event from the window of their family home. She calls Kolja and asks if she has to come out and help. He assures her it will be fine. The car drives off, takes a left and disappears from sight.
