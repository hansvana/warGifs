His wife Larissa looks for him in the entire Lugansk area. She even travels to Rostov in Russia. Without any luck her search ends. Four months later Kolja’s body is found close to his home in Lugansk. Presumably he was killed just days after his disappearance. Due to torture Kolja’s face is unrecognizable. The family recognizes Kolja only by his suit and ring. His whereabouts those past months and what happened to him during that time remains a mystery until this day.
